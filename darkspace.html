Welcome to the world of Dark Space

Join Jolsh on his thrilling adventures through the galaxy, as he faces danger, makes new friends, and uncovers the secrets of the universe. Experience the captivating story with stunning visuals and an incredible soundtrack!

Watch all episodes on our YouTube channel.

Episode 1: Memories

In Episode 1, "Memories," Jolsh and Doge escape Earth after a tragic incident involving Jolsh's wife and find themselves heading into space, unsure of their destination. As they journey through space, they encounter various challenges and strange beings in the subsequent episodes.

in Episode 9, "Big Men," a powerful demon summons three ancient monsters to destroy Jolsh and his friends.
